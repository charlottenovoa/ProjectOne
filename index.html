About Me

Charlotte Novoa

Hello! I'm Charlotte Novoa and I am currently a 19 year old sophomore at San Francisco State studying Visual Communication Design (Graphic Design). I like going to concerts, learning new languages, crafting and baking in my free time. I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area and have done all of my schooling here as well.
