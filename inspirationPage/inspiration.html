Naoi Rei

A member of the group Ive, I gained inspiration from the way she posts on Instagram. I love the way she edits her photos and it has inspired me to be more creative in the way I use my social medias. She tends to use a lot of extra graphics and drawings to enhance her photos. I also really like how she uses her digital camera to take a lot of her pictures. It's actually a Japanese specific camera that she has in pink. It's the Sony Cybershot W810 and I really like the quality and style it gives her photos.
